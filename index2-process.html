the process

how we work

While each project is unique in design, we begin each one the same way.

Initial Meeting: At the first meeting, we discuss the project outlook, budget and desired asethetic. Additionally, we discuss a timeline for the project, depending on the work that needs to be done.

Moving Forward: Once a contract is approved, we take necessary steps to obtain measurements of spaces to be designed and gather any additional information needed.


First Design Meeting: We present interior architecture renovations if needed as well as floor plan options. The client will be shown options for furniture, fabrics, lighting, flooring, wall finishes, etc.

Proposal Approval: After the meeting, the client receives a proposal with the items selected. The proposal includes pricing and leadtime for approval.

Installation: When all items on order arrive, we coordinate and manage the install using our moving team and installers to create a Cashmere-approved space that the client will love and enjoy for years to come.
